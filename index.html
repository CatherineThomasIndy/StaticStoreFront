Hyrule News

His Majesty The King announced today that Hyrule's alliance with the Gerudo Valley had been solidified when Lord Ganondorf pledged his allegiance to His Majesty.

Until this point, little has been known about Gerudo Valley. Any man who has entered before has been imprisoned by the Gerudo. Rumours say that Gerudo Town is full of beautiful (and powerful) women. In fact, legends say that a man is only born once every hundred years, and that he is destined to be their king as Ganondorf is now.

In other news, travelers have reported an increase in obstacles on the road to Kakariko village--more specifically, monsters. Anyone who is planning to leave Hyrule Castle Town is advised to be wary of Peahats during the day. A guard posted by the drawbridge had this to say: "It's imperative that travelers reach a safe destination before nightfall. Stalchilds roam Hyrule field at night." Citizens are warned that the drawbridge closes every night at dusk before the stalchilds can enter the town.
